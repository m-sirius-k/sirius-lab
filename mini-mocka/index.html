Governance Primitives

Personal Audit Trail

Every AI-assisted decision is logged with full context. Searchable, exportable, and tamper-evident. Your work, verifiable.

Decision Ledger

An append-only record of every significant AI-assisted decision. Why you made it, what context you had, what the AI contributed.

AI Interaction Governance

Define rules for how AI can assist your work. Enforce them locally. Know when you're operating within bounds and when you're not.

Local-first, Private

All data stays on your machine. No cloud, no accounts, no vendor visibility into your work. Full data sovereignty.
