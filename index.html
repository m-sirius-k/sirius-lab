FAQ

Common questions

What does "no API key required" mean? +
Orchestra uses the browser sessions you already have — your ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, and Perplexity logins. You don't need to purchase API keys from OpenAI or Anthropic.
Where is my data stored? +
All conversation logs are written only to your browser's IndexedDB. Orchestra has no server — external transmission is physically impossible.
Could my prompts be exposed when sent to each AI? +
Honestly: yes, in the same way as using those AI services directly. Never send confidential business data to any AI tool.
How is this different from ChatHub or Sider? +
ChatHub and Sider route your queries through their own servers. Orchestra has no such relay. Your browser communicates directly with each AI.
Is the 500-copy Launch Edition limit real? +
Yes. Once 500 copies are sold, the Gumroad price switches to $10/mo. Early buyers keep $8/mo for the life of their subscription.
What does the Free plan include? +
Free includes conversation saving, search, and CSV/JSON export — forever, with no time limit.
